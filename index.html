Apache Wilds is a band from Redondo Beach, CA. Iris sings, Daniel plays bass, Anthony plays keys, Ben plays drums, and Michael plays guitar, sings, and writes the songs.

Apache Wilds seamlessly melds post-punk, psychedelic, pop, surf, blues rock, and whatever the hell else they feel like into their own unique style. Songs range from the joyful and celebratory to the wistful and bittersweet, with a healthy dollop of the theatrical and demented. Come watch as they bring back live improvisation to the indie scene.
