Apache Wilds is a band from Redondo Beach, CA. Iris sings, Daniel plays bass, Anthony plays keys, Ben plays drums, and Michael plays guitar and does vox.

Apache Wilds seamlessly melds post-punk, psychedelic, pop, surf, blues rock, and whatever the hell else they feel like into their own unique style.
